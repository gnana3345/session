Explore Paderu

Paderu is a serene hill station nestled in the Eastern Ghats of Andhra Pradesh. Known for its lush greenery, tribal culture, and breathtaking landscapes, it is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

From trekking to waterfalls, and vibrant festivals, Paderu offers a unique blend of tranquility and excitement.
