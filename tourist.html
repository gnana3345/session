Tourist Places Near Paderu

Tourist Places Near Paderu

Must-Visit Places

  • Chaparai Waterfalls - A stunning waterfall located in the hills, perfect for a day trip.
  • Araku Valley - A picturesque valley surrounded by coffee plantations, ideal for nature lovers.
  • Tribal Museum - Explore local handicrafts, traditional arts, and the vibrant life of Paderu's indigenous communities.
  • Coffee Plantations - Visit the lush coffee plantations that are a staple of Paderu's economy.
